triangle

triangle, a Python code which computes properties of a triangle in 2D, including angles, area, centroid, circumcircle, edge lengths, incircle, orientation, orthocenter, and quality.

These properties include:

  • Angles;
  • Area;
  • Centroid;
  • Circumcircle (center and radius);
  • Edge lengths;
  • Incircle (center and radius);
  • Orientation;
  • Orthocenter;
  • Quality: ratio of incircle radius / circumcircle radius;
