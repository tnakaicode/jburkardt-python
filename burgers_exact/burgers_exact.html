burgers_exact

burgers_exact, a Python code which evaluates several exact solutions u(x,t) of the time-dependent 1D viscous Burgers equation.

The form of the Burgers equation considered here is: 

        du       du        d^2 u
        -- + u * -- = nu * -----
        dt       dx        dx^2
for -1.0 < x < +1.0, and 0.0 < t.
