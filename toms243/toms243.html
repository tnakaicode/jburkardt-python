toms243

toms243, a Python library which evaluates the logarithm of a complex value, by David Collens. This is a version of ACM TOMS algorithm 243.

The text of many ACM TOMS algorithms is available online through ACM: http://calgo.acm.org/ or NETLIB: http://www.netlib.org/toms/index.html.
