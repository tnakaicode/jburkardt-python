minimal_surface_exact

minimal_surface_exact, a Python code which evaluates exact solutions u(x,y) to the minimal surface equation: (1+Ux^2) Uyy - 2 Ux Uy Uxy + (1+Uy^2) Uxx = 0.

Four exact solutions are provided here, the plane, the helicoid, the catenoid, and Scherk's function.
